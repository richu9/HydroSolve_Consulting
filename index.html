About Us

Kenya's Trusted Water Professionals

Hydro-Solve Consulting is a Kenya-registered water consultancy firm specializing in comprehensive hydrological services. We serve both public and private sector clients across the country, delivering accurate, regulation-compliant water resource solutions.

Our work is guided by the Water Act 2016 (Cap. 372) and the Water (Resources) Regulations, 2025, as mandated by the Water Resources Authority (WRA). Every project we undertake meets the highest standards of professionalism and regulatory compliance.

Our Services
What We Do

Our Professional Services

Comprehensive hydrological and water resource solutions tailored to meet Kenya's regulatory standards and your project needs.

Hydrological Site Mapping

Comprehensive site mapping to identify water sources, drainage patterns, catchment boundaries, and hydrological features critical for project planning.

Data Collection & Analysis

Systematic hydrological data collection, processing, and advanced analysis including rainfall, river flow, groundwater levels, and water quality parameters.

Desk Study Reviews

Thorough review of existing hydrological records, geological data, land use maps, and historical water resource documentation for informed decision-making.

Water Resources Evaluation

In-depth assessment of surface water and groundwater availability, sustainability analysis, and resource allocation recommendations for responsible use.

Assessment Reports & Surveys

Professional hydrological assessment reports, survey reports, and factsheets developed to WRA standards for permit applications and advocacy.

Dam & Lagoon Design

Structural dam and lagoon designs with complete engineering drawings, hydrological computations, and documentation for construction and regulatory approval.

Survey & Topographical Mapping

Precise topographical surveys and mapping drawings for dam sites, catchment areas, and water infrastructure projects using professional methodologies.

Water Permits & Authorizations

Expert consultation and hands-on assistance with WRA water permit applications, authorizations, and complete processing of all required documentation.

Regulatory Framework

Full Regulatory Compliance

Every project we deliver adheres strictly to Kenya's water sector legal and regulatory frameworks.

Water Act 2016 (Cap. 372)

All our services comply with the Water Act 2016 which provides the legal framework for the management, conservation, use and control of water resources in Kenya.

Water Resources Regulations, 2025

Our work is aligned with the latest Water (Resources) Regulations, 2025 governing permit issuance, water resource use, and environmental protection measures.

Water Resources Authority (WRA)

We operate as mandated by the WRA — Kenya's lead agency responsible for the regulation and management of water resources at the national level.
